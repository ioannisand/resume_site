About Me

Always having been passionate about problem solving and algorithms, during the third year of my biotechnology studies, I was introduced to Python, as part of a university course on programming. Its simple syntax offered a friendly introduction to programming, and I took advantage of that and started learning it, and growing accustomed to some of its powerful tools and useful libraries and frameworks. My goal is to enter the world of professional use of this beautiful and powerful tool to do what drives me, solve problems and offer solutions, as well as develop my skillset.
